About

Cash Back Tracker is a tool used to track the current amount of cash-back rates for some of the most common credit cards in various categories. The categories listed can be thought of as umbrella terms, as the actual category being offered by a certain card is a subset of that term (i.e Card A is listed as 5% cashback in "Food", but Card A's actual offer is specifically for "Grocery Stores" and does not include "Fast-Food/Restaurants"). These categories are designed to help determine which credit card will yield the most cash back according to usage.

  • For full terms and conditions on any credit card listed, please visit the website linked by pressing the "More Info" button on the home page or in the card name title in the cards page.
  • Certain credit cards listed offer rotating 5% cashback categories. These offers are also tracked for their respective cards when possible.
