About

Cash Back Tracker is a tool used to track the current amount of cash-back rates for some of the most common credit cards in various categories. The categories listed can be thought of as umbrella terms, as the actual category being offered by a certain card is a subset of that term (i.e Card A is listed as 5% cashback in "Food", but Card A's actual offer is specifically for "Grocery Stores" and does not include "Fast-Food/Restaurants"). These categories can still help give you a general idea of which card can be used to get the most out of your cashback offers.

  • For full terms and information on any credit card listed, please visit the website linked by pressing the "More Info" button on the home page or in the card name title in the cards page.
  • Some of the credit cards listed offer rotating 5% cashback categories. These offers are also tracked for their respective cards when possible.
